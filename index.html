Gameplay

The player controls a tank and shoot projectiles to destroy enemy tanks around the playfield. The enemy tanks enter from the top of the screen and attempts to destroy player's tank itself.

Multiplayer

Currently you can run several applications on the same computer. Ability to connect from another one is under construction.

How to play

Use arrows to move.

Press space to shoot.
